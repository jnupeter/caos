Help

  • What happens if I do not pay my fees?
    Your application will not be processed until payment is received.
  • What happens if I do not provide my documents?
    Your application will not be processed until documents are received.
  • What happens if I do not meet the minimum entry requirements for my course preference?
    You are not eligible for a place in that course. You need to meet the requirements or change your preferences.
  • What does it mean when it says there have been no offers made?
    If there have been no offers, that course has not yet had an offer round. Check the course offer dates.
